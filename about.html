Mission

To encourage children and young people to practice and develop new skills in technological areas. Creating a fun and friendly system in which they can test their creativity and ability to design innovative projects according to their tastes and preferences, as well as keep them informed of current trends.

Vision

To be an additional support in the education of our new generations, seeking to train our participants in areas of technological-digital evolution and growth. Give them new tools and skills that they can use in the application of projects, as well as open the doors to new development opportunities and work experience. Highlighting our commitment to teaching and the importance of human value. That they share our ideals of healthy coexistence, commitment to education, social inclusion, practice of values, continuous improvement and innovation.

What We Value

  • Professional Ethics
  • Commitment
  • Adaptability
  • Inclusion
  • Social Responsibility
  • Respect
  • Motivation
